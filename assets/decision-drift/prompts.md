# GPT Image 提示词

用户请求：`gpt-image-2`。本次按用户确认的 ChatGPT 订阅路径使用 Codex 内置图像生成能力；该工具不返回模型快照，`provenance.json` 不对其臆测。五张图只做编辑插图，不放文字，避免由模型渲染中文；实际尺寸、哈希和必要的重采样记录也写入该文件。

所有提示词都共有以下排除项：无文字、字母、数字、标志、水印、前后对比、理想脸型等级、侵入式医疗操作、倒计时、放射树、同心外环，以及对 Cognitive Bias Codex 的复刻。

## 总图

Use case: infographic-diagram

Asset type: editorial visual companion for a Chinese consumer psychology book

Primary request: an original conceptual illustration of four distinct translucent doorways around a small central mirror, each doorway representing a different source of decision drift in aesthetic-medicine consumption: information overload, premature interpretation, urgency pressure, and rewritten memory

Style/medium: refined editorial paper-cut illustration, subtle medical-journal restraint, visible warm paper grain, thin charcoal linework, no photorealism

Composition/framing: square composition, four asymmetrical architectural doorways at the corners pointing toward the central mirror, generous negative space, calm hierarchy

Color palette: warm ivory paper and ink charcoal, with all four fixed system accents present: muted teal #5E8B7E, dusty plum #A97892, clay terracotta #C9835A, softened mustard-olive #9B9A66

Constraints: original composition; no radial dendrogram or concentric rings; do not imitate or reproduce the Cognitive Bias Codex; no words, letters, numbers, logos, watermark, before-and-after framing, ideal-face ranking, invasive medical procedures, countdowns, price tags, or human faces
